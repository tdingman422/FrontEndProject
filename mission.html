Our Mission

Our mission is to create high-quality software that changes the world for the better and to do so in a way that is best for everyone.

How We're Achieving It

We are achieving this mission by creating new software products that push the boundries of what computers can do; while maintaining some of our older software in order to make it follow modern standards. We also make sure that we're keeping the planet safe by making sure that our offices are energy efficient and taking the time to research how to keep our practices renewable. We also regularly donate to the community in order to help maintain public services, so that everyone has a chance of success.
